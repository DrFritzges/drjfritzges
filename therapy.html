Therapy

“I think that the greatest gift we can give to each other is our honest self.” – Carl Rogers

Welcome to my practice. I'm Dr. Jessica Fritzges, a licensed psychologist who offers compassionate individual therapy and psychological evaluations to adults to treat and diagnose complex trauma, PTSD, depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, life transitions, grief, dissociation, personality disorders, and more.

Individual therapy…

…is a place where you can begin to address the unique challenges and emotions that have brought you to therapy. In a confidential space that meets you where you are, I provide personalized care to help you learn the skills necessary to manage your emotions, sort out life challenges, and help you feel better.

In every initial appointment, new patients can expect a comprehensive evaluation of their strengths, challenges, identified goals, and opportunities for growth. After a careful assessment that honors your uniqueness and considers your areas of concern, I offer you feedback and a roadmap of how to use therapy to reach your goals.

…A genuine relationship…

Working from a collaborative perspective where all needs and values are honored, I offer an opportunity for genuine connection and growth in our sessions together. For those that may benefit, I offer specialized treatment for complex trauma and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), including eye movement desensitization and reprocessing (EMDR) and trauma-focused cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), as well as a phased approach to treating dissociative disorders.

In the past, I've supported survivors of childhood trauma and abuse, veterans, first responders, healthcare workers, as well as those managing histories of substance use and addiction. When people are seeking care for chronic, personality-based challenges, I incorporate principles from dialectical behavior therapy (DBT), and using a relational approach to work with attachment concerns.

…Delivering compassionate care.

I believe that therapy is most beneficial when there is mutual respect, understanding, and a basis of safety from which to work; to make that possible, I offer care in a nonjudgmental atmosphere that values each person's worth and dignity, seeking to comprehensively address all the aspects that make you who you are.

As a psychologist in independent practice, I offer the opportunity to connect directly for care that is personal, confidential, genuine, and compassionate. To learn more and schedule an initial appointment to meet, please use the scheduling request feature in the patient portal to share a bit about your needs, and start the conversation about what we can achieve for you by working together.
