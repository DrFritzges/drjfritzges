Welcome to my practice. I'm Dr. Jessica Fritzges, a licensed psychologist who offers compassionate individual therapy and psychological evaluations to adults to treat and diagnose complex trauma, PTSD, depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, life transitions, grief, dissociation, personality disorders, and more.

Individual therapy…

…is a place where you can begin to address the unique challenges and emotions that have inspired you to seek support. In a confidential space that meets you where you are, I provide personalized care to help you learn the skills necessary to manage your emotions, sort out life challenges, and help you feel better.

In every initial appointment, new patients can expect a comprehensive evaluation of their strengths, challenges, identified goals, and opportunities for growth. After a careful assessment that honors your uniqueness and considers your areas of concern, I offer you feedback and a roadmap for how therapy can help you reach your goals.

…A genuine relationship…

Working from a collaborative perspective where all needs and values are honored, I offer an opportunity for genuine connection and growth in our sessions together. For those who may benefit, I offer specialized treatment for complex trauma and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), including Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), trauma-focused Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and a phased approach to treating dissociative disorders that makes the work possible and fruitful. When people are seeking care for chronic, personality-based challenges, I incorporate principles from Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), while using a relational approach to work with attachment concerns. Ultimately, regardless of treatment approach, I help patients envision what is possible, and take steps to enact those changes.

... With specialty trauma care...

While I provide therapy for a wide range of emotional and psychological concerns, a significant focus of my practice is helping adults recover from the effects of complex trauma and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).

I work with individuals whose experiences may include childhood abuse or neglect, sexual assault, narcissistic or coercively controlling relationships, traumatic loss, serious accidents, natural disasters, military service, first responder experiences, and other overwhelming life events.

For individuals whose trauma has resulted in dissociation or disruptions in identity, memory, emotional regulation, or relationships, I offer a thoughtful, phased approach that emphasizes safety, stabilization, and long-term recovery.

Every person's story is unique, and treatment is tailored to your individual experiences, strengths, goals, and pace of healing.

…Delivering compassionate care.

I believe that therapy is most beneficial when there is mutual respect, understanding, and a basis of safety from which to work. To make that possible, I offer care in a nonjudgmental atmosphere that values each person's worth and dignity, seeking to comprehensively address all the aspects that make you who you are.

As a psychologist in independent practice, I offer the opportunity to connect directly for care that is personal, confidential, genuine, and compassionate. To learn more and schedule an initial appointment, please use the scheduling request feature in the patient portal to share a bit about your needs and begin the conversation about what we can achieve by working together.
