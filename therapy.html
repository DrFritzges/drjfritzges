Welcome to my practice. I’m Dr. Jessica Fritzges, a licensed psychologist offering individual psychotherapy and psychological evaluation for adults experiencing trauma, PTSD, anxiety, depression, bipolar disorder, grief, dissociation, personality-related concerns, and life transitions.

Individual therapy

Individual therapy is a place to begin understanding what you have been carrying emotionally, cognitively, and physically.

In a confidential and collaborative space, we work together to identify patterns, strengthen coping strategies, and address the underlying concerns contributing to distress.

Initial sessions include a comprehensive evaluation of your strengths, current challenges, history, and goals. From this, I provide feedback and a clear direction for treatment.

A collaborative therapeutic relationship

Therapy is grounded in trust, safety, and collaboration.

My approach is relational and evidence-informed, drawing from trauma-focused CBT, EMDR-informed interventions, and skills-based approaches such as DBT when helpful.

Treatment is individualized and may focus on emotional regulation, trauma processing, identity integration, and attachment patterns.

I have worked with adults impacted by childhood trauma, abuse, complex PTSD, dissociation, as well as veterans, first responders, healthcare professionals, and individuals recovering from substance use or high-stress environments.

Specialized trauma care

While I provide therapy for a wide range of emotional and psychological concerns, a significant focus of my practice is helping adults recover from the effects of complex trauma and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD).

I work with individuals whose experiences may include childhood abuse or neglect, sexual assault, narcissistic or coercively controlling relationships, traumatic loss, serious accidents, natural disasters, military service, first responder experiences, and other overwhelming life events.

For individuals whose trauma has resulted in dissociation or disruptions in identity, memory, emotional regulation, or relationships, I offer a thoughtful, phased approach that emphasizes safety, stabilization, and long-term recovery.

Every person's story is unique, and treatment is tailored to your individual experiences, strengths, goals, and pace of healing.

Compassionate, respectful care

Therapy is most effective when it is grounded in respect, emotional safety, and a nonjudgmental stance.

My goal is to create a space where you can explore difficult experiences without fear of being dismissed or misunderstood.

As an independent practitioner, I offer direct, individualized care focused on depth, consistency, and meaningful progress.
